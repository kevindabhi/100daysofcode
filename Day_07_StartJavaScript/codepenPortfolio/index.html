About me

Hello, my name is Kevin and I am learning how to build a website. My goal is to be come a digital nomad. My journey with web development started when I was 12, this was the year 2003 and the first time I saw a computer at my cousin's home. In India it was a privilige to have a computer at that time this was a pentium II If I remember correctly, with 108 mhz clock speed and a turbo button which made it go 128Mhz. A year or so later I asked my parents to bring that computer home because no one was using it. We did not have internet at that time, and I had found a book on HTML, all I did was write some code make colorful pages and see new commands but there was nothing new. At that time we had yahoo offline website on the computer and the most important thing I learned at that time was "view/source" I learned from that code, often broken and I had no idea about CSS or why is the website broken (of course because it wasn't online) it certainly was an exciting time.. And then I started engineering in 2007, the dream of building websites, changing the world came crashing down because it was not what I expected. I wasted a decade tryin to pass my engineering and I still couldn't because I could not pass just one maths subject. So 10 years, 46 subjects and a project later. here I am, the same child in 2004 learning it again but this time not for fun, but for survival.
